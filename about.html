Background

Daina K. is a dedicated musician whose love for music began in a family of musicians. With a rich background in classical piano and various instruments, she's honed her skills through formal training and a deep exploration of diverse musical genres. Her teaching philosophy blends technical expertise with a passion for nurturing talent. With over a decade of experience, she's committed to guiding students on their musical journey, aiming to instill the same love and dedication to music that has shaped her life.

Philosophy

  • Individual Creativity: Daina K. values and encourages the individual creative spark within each student. Rather than just focusing on playing notes, she emphasizes the importance of exploring one's unique musical ideas and expressions
  • Deep Connection to Music: Her teaching approach aims to instill a profound connection to music itself. Beyond learning to play an instrument or understand music theory, students are encouraged to develop a deeper emotional and intellectual bond with the art form.
  • Empowerment and Uniqueness: Daina K. empowers her students to discover and embrace their distinct musical voices. She understands and celebrates the diversity in musical preferences, styles, and talents, guiding students toward finding their authentic musical identity.
  • Blend of Technical Expertise and Self-Expression: While technical proficiency is crucial, she harmonizes this expertise with a strong emphasis on self-expression. Students are not just taught to reproduce music mechanically but to infuse it with their emotions, thoughts, and individuality.
